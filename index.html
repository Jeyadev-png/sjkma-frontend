About the Academy

Sri Jai Krishna Music Academy was founded with a clear vision — to preserve and pass on the richness of Indian classical music through disciplined, traditional teaching.

For over two decades, students of all age groups have trained here, building strong musical foundations, confidence, and a deep respect for classical art forms.

Why Learn With Us

Every student receives personal guidance with structured progression. The focus is on strong fundamentals, clarity in learning, and long-term musical growth — not shortcuts.
